Three things you will find in Canada:
  1. Poutine
  2. Lumberjacks
  3. Hockey players

Lumberjack is a historical term, associated with folklore and of times past, to refer to what we today call 'loggers'. The term lumberjack is first credited to be used in reference to a group of unruly Canadian men who made a living working in the wild forest, and could not be subjected to the constraints of civil law.
